    Get Involved now!

    We’re glad you’re here!

    There are three ways that you can get involved at WSU via gciConnect!:

    1. Research Involvement

    Everyone is aging! We are living longer than ever before for the first time in the history of the world!

    The population shift will change our society and WSU researches are actively working to answer the many specific questions about how we will adapt, such as:


    • How can we support people to age with dignity?

    • How can older adults stay engaged and connected?

    • How will family relationships between older and younger generations be affected by increased longevity?

    •How will we design and deliver services that best support older adults and their families?

    •How will our communities adapt to an aging population?


    WSU researchers may need your opinion and perspective in addressing these research questions. Your participation in research can help others and contributes to advancing knowledge about aging. If you’d like to explore some of the current research labs focused on aging research, check here.

    2. Education & Mentorship

    WSU’s traditional slogan of “World Class, Face to Face” highlights the university’s commitment to helping students become more aware, engaged, creative, and skilled at addressing the big issues and problems of the world.

    Preparing for an aging society is a pressing social issue and gciConnect! aims to leverage the expertise and experience of older people, support lifelong learning, and promote positive intergenerational connections.


    • Do you have special skills, knowledge, or background/training that you want to share with WSU students or faculty?

    • Do you want to connect with students to enhance their learning and stay engaged with a campus community (e.g., mentorship opportunities, guest speaker opportunities; participating in life history projects with undergraduate students)?


    We hope you will get involved with gciConnect! through Education and Mentorship opportunities!


    3. Outreach & Charitable Contributions

    Engaging with community partners to address the needs of our changing world is a strategic goal of Washington State University and Granger Cobb Institute for Senior Living.


    • Do you want to stay informed of current Granger Cobb Institute for Senior Living initiatives, research, and projects?

    • Are you interested in funding experiential learning projects to support students or creating scholarships for students who pursue education, training, and careers that support older adults and their families?


    Contact us with additional questions or, if you’re ready to get involved, please submit your contact information and we’ll follow up with you soon.

    Thank you in advance for your time and important contributions. We look forward to working with you!
