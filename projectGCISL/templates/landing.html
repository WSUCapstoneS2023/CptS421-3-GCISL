Welcome to gciConnect!

GCI Connect is part of Washington State University’s (WSU) Granger Cobb Institute for Senior Living (GCI) and serves as an innovative resource to keep the community informed and connected to WSU.


WSU’s GCI is among the few universities in the nation that offers a Senior Living Management major to:

• Promote the field of senior living management as a rewarding career path for students.
• Partner with industry leaders to help maintain current evidence-based practices.
• Help improve the quality of life for older adults in Washington and beyond.
